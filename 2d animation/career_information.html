Career Information


After completing an animation training program , you can be able to seek a job as an animator. Unless you complete your animation training program, you cannot be able to do anything related to 2D animation.

What 2D animator can do?

1. They can develop storyboards i.e a sequence of drawing, typically with some directions and dialogue, representing the shots planned for a film or television production.
2. They can design characters, backgrounds, and scene transitions, bringing creative ideas and concepts to life.
3. They can arrange characters and items in a scene.
4. They use computers to create graphic, motion and animation.
5. After receiving views and feedbacks from clients they can edit animations.

Animators create animations and visual effects for flims, video games, television, mobile devices and others forms of media. For creating such animations they use illustrations and software programs. Adobe after effects, adobe premier etc are just few leading software programs for animators.

They create plan, and script animated narrative sequences, and assist with background design and production coordination. You could work for large or small animation studio, computer ganes developers, interactive media designers, or post-production houses for animated film and Tv titles and credits. Freelance fixed-term contracts are common. The main centres for animation in the UK are London, Bristol, Cardiff, Dundee and Msnchester. CGI is the fastest growing sector of animation, but there is still a demand for people with good drawing skills. The more skills you can offer, the more employment options you will have.

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), employment of all animators and multimedia artists (including those working in 2D animation) is expected to increase by six percent from 2014-2024, which is slower than the national average for all careers. The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) noted that the median annual wage for fine artists and illustrators was $43,890 in 2014, while multimedia artist and animators earns $63.630.
